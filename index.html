"The Cat Box" A literal Rick Roll

The 'Rickrolling' meme itself is incredibly old and famous. It's universal on the internet, often done through a link to something that people would be excited to see; cat pictures, something funny, interesting information or the promise of something incredible caught on camera. When they click the link theyre greeted with the music video and song of "never gonna give you up"

The entire concept of 'rick-rolling' is something so exclusive to the internet. Navigating the internet is done entirely through links, so it's incredibly easy to just fake a link that takes them to something completely irrelevant. We are completely blind to what's on the other side of a link until we actually click it. In the real world we don't really have this problem, we move somewhere and we can see where we're going. We interact with something, we see what it is before even touching it. This "Cat Box" is a recreation of that in a real world form.

Cats were used as the 'bait' to lure people into opening the box. Cat pictures while harmless are also a massive part of the internet, so it seemed fitting to decorate the outside as such.
